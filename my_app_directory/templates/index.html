What Is MyClean?

MyClean is an innovative platform designed to streamline the booking management process for individual cleaners and cleaning companies with a user-friendly website. MyClean offers a seamless experience for customers seeking cleaning services.


The app allows users to easily browse current deals, schedule appointments, and receive reminders, all for free. Service providers can benefit from a range of features, including a job intake form, customer information intake system, and appointment confirmation tools.


The platform also includes a secure provider onboarding process with a security check and a supervisor dashboard for effective management. Additionally, MyClean offers job opportunities for cleaners and cleaning companies, making it an essential tool for both customers and service providers.

How Much Is MyClean?


MyClean is free for all customers. MyClean takes a very small % cut from all service providers revenue.


How Do I Become A Service Provider?


Becoming a service provider is easy. Simply sign up as one, complete the relevant security checks and submit your details. Once your approved your good to go.

How Do I Book A Cleaning Service?


Customers can book a cleaning service directly through a service provider by contacting them. In addition, job postings can also be made.


Can I Cancel My Appointment?


Yes, you can cancel an appointment at any time. However, cancellations that are not made at least two business days in advance will incur a late fee.
